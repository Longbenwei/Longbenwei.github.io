Features

LEVI is here to tackle this challenge.

  • Multi-parameter assessment

    LEVI is a powerful tool that provides a wide range of information, including important patient monitoring parameters, diagnostic measurement data, and assistive decision-making tools, to help you assess patients of varying severity levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main function of LEVI?

LEVI is a multi-function defibrillator and monitor that combines defibrillation, ECG monitoring, pulse oximetry, and non-invasive blood pressure measurement, suitable for emergency and in-hospital monitoring.

How do I operate the LEVI defibrillator?

First, make sure the electrode pads are properly attached to the patient's chest. Turn on the device and select the appropriate energy level. Then press the defibrillation button to perform the defibrillation. The device will guide the user through the entire process.

LEVI What defibrillation modes are supported?

It supports manual defibrillation and automatic external defibrillation (AED) mode, and users can choose the appropriate mode based on the situation.

How do I replace the battery and charge it?

Open the battery compartment cover, remove the old battery, and insert the new one. Use the specified charger to charge the battery, and the charging indicator light will show the charging status.

Does the equipment have a self-checking function?

Yes, LEVI has an automatic self-diagnostic function that checks the device daily, weekly, and monthly to ensure it's always available.

How do you maintain and service equipment?

Regularly check the status of the equipment and electrode pads, clean the housing and screen, ensure that the battery is fully charged, and follow the manufacturer's maintenance manual for detailed maintenance.
