Convenient Sharing, Connected Care
 
You can share patient data throughout the care continuum and across multiple applications. With just one button press, HeartStart Intrepid can collect vital signs, a 12-lead ECG, and an event summary, and send them to the Philips Emergency Care Information Suite. By storing critical data in the cloud, you can: Update the patient's medical record - use the emergency care report to connect to your hospital's electronic medical record to retrieve data Obtain a complete event document. Automated reporting - the Emergency Care Information Suite includes event analysis reports, such as ECGs before and after defibrillation, to support hospital quality management. Store data - for post-event clinical data analysis to identify trends and drive improvements.
 
 
Design for change, easy to manage. Integrate data, continuously advance.
 
The Philips cloud platform hosts the emergency care information, so there is no need to invest in expensive hardware or build infrastructure, no need to maintain servers, and your IT resources can be devoted to other critical projects.
The Emergency Care Coordination Information System can be launched and run without the need for a client software installation. Upgrades allow for easy access to the latest features. Users can easily access patient information from their preferred web browser, allowing them to assist with their work.
You and your team have access to detailed event reports stored in the Emergency Care Information Suite, which is suitable for nursing documentation and post-event review. Integrated analytical tools can easily create reports to measure nursing quality, thereby driving the organization's quality management and motivating continuous improvement.
